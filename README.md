# Inventory dashboard 
📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Sales and Inventory Dashboard designed to monitor key business metrics and support data-driven decision-making. The dashboard provides insights into sales performance, product trends, regional analysis, and inventory management.

It helps businesses track performance efficiently and identify opportunities for growth and optimization.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze sales performance across different time periods
Identify top-performing products and stores
Monitor inventory levels and stock conditions
Understand regional sales distribution
Evaluate customer purchase behavior

📊 Key KPIs & Analysis
🔹 Sales Performance
MTD (Month-to-Date) – Sales from start of month till date
QTD (Quarter-to-Date) – Sales from start of quarter till date
YTD (Year-to-Date) – Sales from start of year till date
Sales Growth – Measure increase/decrease in sales over time
Daily Sales Trend – Identify patterns and fluctuations

🔹 Product & Store Analysis
Product-wise Sales – Top and low-performing products
Top 5 Stores – Best-performing stores based on sales
Purchase Method-wise Sales – Online, In-store, Cash, Card

🔹 Regional Analysis
State-wise Sales – Performance by state
Region-wise Sales – Overall regional insights

📦 Inventory Analysis
Total Inventory – Available stock across all locations
Inventory Value – Total worth of inventory

📌 Inventory Status
🟢 In Stock – Inventory meets required level
🟡 Under Stock – Below desired level
🔴 Out of Stock – No inventory available

🛠️ Tools & Technologies

Power BI – Data visualization & dashboard creation

SQL – Data querying and analysis

Excel – Data cleaning and preprocessing

📈 Dashboard Features
Interactive filters and slicers
KPI cards for quick insights
Time-based trend analysis
Drill-down by product, region, and store
Inventory status tracking

🚀 Business Impact
Improved sales monitoring and analysis
Better inventory control and planning
Identification of growth opportunities
Enhanced decision-making using data insights

📌 Conclusion

This dashboard demonstrates how data can be transformed into actionable insights to improve business performance, optimize inventory, and support strategic decisions










🚚## Supply Chain Analytics Dashboard

📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on building an interactive Supply Chain Analytics Dashboard to monitor and optimize end-to-end supply chain operations. It provides insights into order management, inventory levels, procurement costs, logistics performance, and demand forecasting.

The dashboard helps businesses improve efficiency, reduce operational costs, and make data-driven decisions.

🎯 Objectives
Track order and sales performance
Monitor inventory and stock health
Analyze procurement and supply chain costs
Evaluate logistics and delivery efficiency
Improve demand forecasting and fulfillment

📊 Key KPIs & Analysis
🔹 1. Order & Sales KPIs
Total Orders → COUNT(Order_ID)
Total Sales Revenue → SUM(Unit_Price × Quantity)
Average Order Value (AOV) → Revenue ÷ Total Orders
Orders by Region/Country/City → Geographic sales insights

📦 2. Inventory & Stock KPIs
Stock on Hand → Total available inventory
Reorder Status → % of products below reorder level
Average Lead Time → Average days to restock
Inventory Turnover Ratio → Total Cost ÷ Stock on Hand

💰 3. Procurement & Cost KPIs
Procurement Cost → Total purchasing cost
Transportation Cost → Shipping/logistics cost
Total Supply Chain Cost → Overall operational cost
Cost per Unit → Total Cost ÷ Quantity

🚛 4. Logistics & Delivery KPIs
On-Time Delivery % → Orders delivered on schedule
Average Delay (Days) → Delay in deliveries
Orders by Ship Mode → Standard, Express, etc.
Transport Mode Utilization → Orders by transport type

📈 5. Demand & Fulfillment KPIs
Forecast Accuracy → Accuracy of demand prediction
Fill Rate → Fulfilled demand vs total demand
Backorder Rate → Orders delayed due to low stock
Demand vs Actual Sales → Forecast vs actual trend

🛠️ Tools & Technologies
Power BI – Interactive dashboard & visualization
SQL – Data extraction and KPI calculations
Excel – Data cleaning and preprocessing

📈 Dashboard Features
Dynamic filters and slicers
KPI cards for quick performance tracking
Drill-down by region, product, and transport mode
Trend analysis for demand and sales
Real-time supply chain performance monitoring

🚀 Business Impact
Improved inventory planning and stock control
Reduced supply chain and logistics costs
Enhanced delivery performance
Better demand forecasting accuracy
Stronger data-driven decision-making


📌 Conclusion

This project demonstrates how supply chain data can be transformed into actionable insights to optimize operations, reduce delays, and improve overall efficiency
